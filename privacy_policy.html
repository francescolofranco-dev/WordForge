WordForge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

WordForge is a vocabulary learning app that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Period.

Data Storage

All data you enter into WordForge — words, meanings, progress, and statistics — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your phone.

Data Collection

WordForge does not collect any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal data.

Network Access

WordForge does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. The app works entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

WordForge does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

WordForge requests the following permission:

Notifications: Used to send you word review reminders at scheduled times. This permission is optional — the app works without it, but you won't receive reminders. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual words or all words at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

Children's Privacy

WordForge does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since WordForge collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
